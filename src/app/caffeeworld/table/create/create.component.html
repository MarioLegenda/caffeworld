Create a table

Hello there. You are about to create a table to have some fun with your friends. But before you do, it is important for you to know how we handle your email (or better, not handle it at all!). We need your email only to send the url of the table to you and your friends. After we send those emails, they are not used anymore by the application and they are not in any way saved by the application.

Actually, you don't have to give us your email. The only mandatory field is the nickname. Everything else is optional. After you create the table, you can send the url of the table to your friends with some other medium, like facebook or gmail.

Have fun!
